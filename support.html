Frequently Asked Questions

The app crashed. What should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed and contact us with details about your device and what happened.

How do I report a bug?
Send us an email describing what happened, what you expected, and what device you're using. Screenshots are always helpful.
