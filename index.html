Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

FUAKO ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Pandagame mobile application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Children's Privacy

Pandagame is designed to be suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Our third-party advertising partner operates under COPPA-compliant settings, which disables behavioral targeting and cross-app tracking.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. However, our app uses Unity Ads, a third-party advertising service, which may collect the following data to serve contextual ads:

  • Device identifiers — used for ad serving, not for cross-app tracking
  • Coarse location — derived from IP address, used for region-appropriate ad delivery
  • Ad interaction data — such as ad views and clicks, used to measure ad performance
  • Diagnostics — crash logs and performance data, used to maintain app stability

How Information Is Used

The data collected by Unity Ads is used for:

  • Serving contextual, age-appropriate advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance and analytics
  • Maintaining app stability and diagnosing crashes

No data is used for behavioral targeting, user profiling, or cross-app tracking.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party service:

  • Unity Ads — for displaying advertisements. You can review their privacy policy at unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties, except as described in relation to Unity Ads above.

Data Retention

We do not store any user data on our servers. Any data collected by Unity Ads is subject to their data retention policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete data associated with your use of the app. Since we do not collect personal data directly, such requests should be directed to Unity Ads through their privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
